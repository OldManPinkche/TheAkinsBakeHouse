Local note

The Akins Bake House is local to Tryon, Oklahoma and does not ship or take out-of-state orders. Bakes are made in a home kitchen that may handle wheat, dairy, eggs, peanuts, and tree nuts.

About The Baker

Hi, I’m Taylor, the baker behind The Akins Bake House.

Baking has always been more than making desserts. It is my way of bringing people together around something warm, thoughtful, and made with care.

The Akins Bake House started as a home baking dream and grew into a way to serve my local community with treats that feel personal and comforting.

Every cookie, cake pop, pie, cinnamon roll, loaf of bread, and bowl of banana pudding is made from scratch with quality ingredients and attention to detail.

Thank you for supporting this small bake house and letting me be part of the memories you make around the table.
