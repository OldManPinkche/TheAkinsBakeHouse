About The Baker

Hi, I’m Taylor, the baker behind Akins Bakehouse!

Baking has always been more than just making desserts. It’s my way of bringing people together. There’s something special about watching someone smile after taking that first bite of a warm cinnamon roll, a homemade cookie, or a slice of pie. That’s the feeling I hope to share with every order.

Akins Bakehouse was born from a season of life that challenged me to find a new path. After being diagnosed with Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome (POTS), I realized that working from home was the best choice for my health. Instead of giving up on having a career I loved, I chose to turn my passion for baking into something I could share with my community.

Alongside running my bakery, I also work as a Personal Care Assistant, caring for a wonderful woman who has reminded me how meaningful it is to serve others with patience, kindness, and compassion. Those same values are at the heart of everything I bake.

At Akins Bakehouse, every cookie, cake pop, pie, cinnamon roll, loaf of bread, and bowl of banana pudding is made from scratch with quality ingredients, attention to detail, and lots of love. As a small, family-owned bakery, I believe every customer deserves personal attention and every order deserves my very best.

When you support Akins Bakehouse, you’re supporting more than a small business. You’re supporting a dream that grew from resilience, faith, and the belief that difficult seasons can lead to beautiful beginnings. Thank you for allowing me to be a small part of your celebrations, family gatherings, and everyday moments. It truly means more to me than you’ll ever know.

I’m so grateful you’re here, and I can’t wait to bake something special for you.
